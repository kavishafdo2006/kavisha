About Us

Welcome to LIQUOR WORLD, where exceptional quality meets unparalleled selection. We are your trusted source for premium spirits, fine wines, craft beers, and more. Our mission is simple: to bring the finest beverages from around the globe straight to your glass, whether you're celebrating life’s big milestones, hosting a casual gathering, or simply unwinding after a long day.

Story
LIQUOR WORLD was founded by Kavisha Fernando, a passionate entrepreneur with a deep appreciation for the art and craft behind exceptional drinks. Recognizing the growing demand for a curated liquor experience, Kavisha envisioned a platform that goes beyond selling beverages—it’s about creating a space where enthusiasts, collectors, and casual drinkers can explore, discover, and enjoy the world of spirits.
What started as a dream has now grown into a trusted destination for thousands of customers who value quality, variety, and personalized service. Kavisha’s dedication to excellence drives every aspect of LIQUOR WORLD, from the carefully curated inventory to the seamless shopping experience we provide. Sets Us Apart
At LIQUOR WORLD, we’re more than just a liquor store—we’re a hub for discovery, education, and celebration. Here’s what makes us unique:
1.Extensive Selection
Our shelves boast a diverse array of spirits, wines, and beers sourced from renowned producers and hidden gems around the world. Whether you're looking for a rare single malt whiskey, a classic Bordeaux, or the latest craft beer release, we’ve got something to excite your palate.
2.Commitment to Quality
We take pride in offering only the finest products. Every item in our collection is handpicked to ensure it meets our rigorous standards for taste, authenticity, and craftsmanship.
3.Expert Guidance
Whether you’re a seasoned connoisseur or a curious beginner, our knowledgeable team is here to help. From pairing recommendations to exclusive product insights, we’re committed to making your experience as enjoyable and informative as possible.
4.Community and Connection
At LIQUOR WORLD, we celebrate the connections that good drinks create. Through events, tastings, and educational content, we aim to build a vibrant community of enthusiasts who share a passion for quality and culture.

Our Vision
We believe that every bottle tells a story, and our vision is to help you find the perfect match for your moment. LIQUOR WORLD is dedicated to elevating the liquor shopping experience by combining convenience, variety, and a personal touch. Whether you’re exploring online or visiting one of our locations, we strive to make your journey memorable.
Giving Back
At LIQUOR WORLD, we’re committed to making a positive impact. From supporting local distilleries and wineries to partnering with charitable organizations, we believe in giving back to the community that inspires us every day.
Join Us
Thank you for choosing LIQUOR WORLD as your trusted destination for premium beverages. We’re excited to share our passion with you and help you discover your next favorite drink. Here’s to creating unforgettable moments and lasting memories, one sip at a time.
